PsychCraft

PsychCraft is an addon that introduces a sanity system into Minecraft, adding a unique twist to survival. A new UI element displays your sanity level, which gradually decreases based on various conditions such as being in the dark, roaming during nighttime, encountering undead mobs, or wielding certain sanity-affecting weapons and armor.
